The Treaty was signed on 6th February 1840 and got its name from the location where it was first signed, Waitangi.

The image above, shows the signing of the Treaty of Waitangi between the British Crown and Māori rangatira (chiefs).

The Treaty of Waitangi is stored at the Reserve Bank in Wellington.
